FAQ

Why do i need deep cleaning before regular cleaning?

Deep cleaning is crucial prior to regular maintenance for several reasons:

  • Elimination of Dirt and Accumulated Residues

    Deep cleaning involves the thorough removal of dirt and accumulated residues, promoting a healthier and cleaner home. While regular cleaning aids in maintaining cleanliness, the removal of ingrained dirt is essential for ensuring efficient regular cleaning.

  • Addressing Hard-to-Reach Areas

    In the process of deep cleaning, overlooked areas - such as behind furniture, inside cabinets, the oven, refrigerator, and other challenging surfaces - are meticulously cleaned. Regular cleaning might miss these areas, but deep cleaning guarantees their thorough attention.

  • Optimizing Efficiency for Regular Cleaning

    Deep cleaning serves as a preparation phase, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of regular maintenance. By eliminating dirt and debris during deep cleaning, the subsequent regular cleaning becomes more manageable, saving time and effort. In essence, deep cleaning is pivotal for removing dirt and debris, cleaning hard-to-reach areas, and priming your home for efficient regular cleaning.
